To make Dark Background and Light Text extension work properly, you should perform following steps:
  • Open Firefox Preferences (Menu â†’ Preferences)
  • in General tab, Language and Appearance paragraph, press Colors... button
  • in a dialog, set Override the colors specified by the page with your selections above to Never
You see this notification because:
  • You have Override the colors specified by the page with your selections above set to Only with High Contrast themes and you have High Contrast theme enabled in your Operating System.
  • OR
  • You have Override the colors specified by the page with your selections above set to Always
Note that situations above completely prevent Dark Background and Light Text from changing page colors therefore it doesn't makes much sense to use the extension at all in such cases.
If none of above circumstances is your case and you use the most recent Firefox version but still see this notification, please, report a bug precisely decribing your case.
